Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to:

  1. MO14.1. Examine the role women play in our lives and their importance to society regarding both the women presented by Pizan and a contemporary example of your choice.  

  2. MO14.2. Apply the concept of redefining feminine virtues in positive and non-binary terms 

  3. MO14.3 Interpret the significance of de Pizan’s use of primary texts (for example, the Bible), to create juxtaposition and refute chauvinists.  

  4. MO14.4 apply and utilize vocabulary highlighted within the texts (also provided on the flashcards) accurately in written responses.
  5. MO14.5 Identify key concepts, plot, and characters for the texts we have covered in Chapter Two.
  6. MO14.6 Produce a concise summary of two texts.
  7. MO14.7 Develop a critical analysis of a chosen text
  8. MO14.8 engage with a text from a critical and contemporary standpoint

  9. MO14.9 Correctly use and cite quotations from a text to support an analysis.
