Dedication

This is dedicated to those who still love to read (I know, old fashioned). As limited animals with unlimited horizons, we are born into a universe that is silent and indifferent. To survive the terror of this void, we build narratives of meaning: Hero Systems that act as the gravity to hold our fragile selves together. These texts are not dead history; they are the scaffolding that built the reality of our imagination. This is for the builders.
