Literary Analysis Topic Proposal

Literary Analysis Topic Proposal

Purpose

The purpose of this proposal is to ensure that you have selected the one ancient text you will analyze for the Literary Analysis (Anthology Project) and that you are on the right track to connect it to a modern issue.

Note: This analysis functions as a forward to your Anthology Project. The text you choose here must also appear in your final anthology, and your proposal should explain why this text belongs and how it connects to the issue you’ve identified.

Task

For a complete score on this task, be sure to provide all of the following information:

  • Title: The proposal title and your name.

  • Summary: A synopsis of the proposed project, including your rationale for choosing this particular text, the modern issue you will connect it to, your specific aims and objectives in the analysis, and the potential significance of the text today.

  • Specific Aims: A clear, point-by-point outline of how you intend to analyze the text in relation to the modern issue.

  • Background and Significance: Briefly explain any background necessary for the chosen text and the modern issue. Discuss why this connection matters and how it will strengthen your Anthology Project.

Criteria

  • Turn in your proposal document to the designated dropbox before the due date.

  • The proposal is required. You cannot earn any points on the Anthology Project without it.

  • Late proposals will lose points according to the syllabus policy.
