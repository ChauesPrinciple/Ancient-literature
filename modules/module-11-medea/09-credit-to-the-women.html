Credit to the Women

Here is a choice of articles providing examples of prominent women who did and achieved amazing things to shape our world as we know it:

Birka Viking, Mabel Stark, Bessie B. Stringfield, Jackie Mitchell, Hedy Lamarr, or Rosalind Franklin.

They were, and are still, tragically overlooked or downplayed in popular media and/or the historical eye. Choose one of the historical figures (or one from outside if you are so inclined, but please have it approved) and discuss why her life and accomplishments speak to you.

Furthermore, reflect on this video from the Haudenosaunee and the story of the Great Law of Peace; specifically, I would like you to focus on the role of Jikonhsaseh in the establishment of peace through the Matriarchal lines. Whose or what "heroic systems"  do you imagine are the causes (then and/or now)?
