Instructor Bio

After my medical discharge from the Army, I earned a B.S. in English and Psychology. After the recession, I returned to earn my master's degree and worked for an in-house publication company while I finished my coursework. After that, I spent time overseas, in Hong Kong and Saudi Arabia, among others. After my father got sick, I moved to Nashville to teach here at NSCC in 2015, and I have been a full-time instructor at NSCC since. I have truly enjoyed my work here for the past five years! I teach English composition and literature courses, and I have a particular fondness for fiction, and I specialize in translated literature. Please see my post on the Introduction Discussion Board for more information about me. I'm always here to answer any questions or help you, so please don't hesitate to contact me. I look forward to getting to know all of you, and I am excited to have you as a student in the first OER course at Nashville State, where the material is written by your professors, not a publishing company.
