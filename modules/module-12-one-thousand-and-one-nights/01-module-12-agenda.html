Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to: 

  1. MO12.1. Explore the implications of how fidelity is applied unequally via gender and power throughout One Thousand and One Nights
  2. MO12.2. Investigate the role that narrative plays in breaking down unfair systems and combatting insecurity
  3. MO12.3 Explore how patriarchal systems allow for double standards and unequal treatment
  4. MO12.4 Examine the role of insecurity and jealousy in the objectification of women.
  5. MO12.5 Review and critique fellow students’ work using the guidelines within the discussion board.
