Anthology Project Brainstorm

﻿

Now that we have spent more time with the content of the course and you have a better understanding of the readings and the approach of this class, use this discussion to brainstorm more in-depth about your Anthology Project. Please consider the following questions:

  1. Do you plan to change the focus of the anthology from the chapter's focus? If so, how?
    • e.g., stories of why people, like medical professionals, are driven to help others.
  2. Which of the stories from our course do you think you will keep?
  3. Which stories do you think you may add?
  4. Do you plan to make additions other than books or stories?
    • e.g., films, songs, poems, plays, etc.

Reflect & Discuss

Engage with the text and your peers using the reading questions above.
