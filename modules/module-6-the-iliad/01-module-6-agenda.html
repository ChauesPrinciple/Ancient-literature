Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to:

  1. MO6.1 Identify specific characteristics honor cultures, guilt cultures, and how they affect interactions and literature.

  2. MO6.2 Compare honor societies, guilt societies, and how that affects Achilles and modern warriors.
  3. MO6.3 Apply Jonathan Shay’s principles of betrayal and rage to the “Code of the Streets”.
  4. MO6.4 Synthesize the concepts of force (from the Analysis for the Iliad), moral injuries, and the “Code of the Streets”.
  5. MO6.6 Compare the reaction of Gilgamesh and Achilles in response to the loss of a close friend.
