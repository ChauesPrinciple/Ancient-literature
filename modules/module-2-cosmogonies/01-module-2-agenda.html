Learning Objectives

When writing learning objectives for a course or module, remember:

  1. MO2.1 apply Ernest Becker’s theory of “Heroic Systems” to the readings for this week (CO 3, 4) 
  2. MO2.2 Compare and contrast our first texts: Works and Days by Hesiod and the Popol Vuh, with the concept of the Veil of the Maya. (CO 1, 2, 3)
  3. MO2.3 compare  the two texts to analyze what is similar between creation stories (CO 1, 2, 3, 4)
  4. MO2.4 Interpret how Heroic Systems help us better understand the role of creation stories. (CO 2, 3, 4)

Learning Activities

  1. Read Introduction to Chapter One, the Background, Summary, and Lecture for Cosmogonies, Hesiod, Works and Days Part 1-3, and Popol Vuh Background, Summary, Book 1 and Book 2. Respond to the question at the end.

  2. This week, please view the following multimedia materials:

    • This video by Crash Course gives a wider view of the purpose of creation myths, some of their common topics, and the meaning inherent in ritual.
  3. Complete the Literary Quick Take and the Anthology Project Point of interest discussion for this week. 

Reflect & Discuss

Engage with the text and your peers using the reading questions above.
