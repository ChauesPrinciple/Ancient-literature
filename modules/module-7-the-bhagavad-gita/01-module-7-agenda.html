Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to:

  1. MO7.1. Investigate how different cultural areas and influences affect what a warrior is and what their virtues are
  2. MO7.2. Determine how a morally aligned warrior (with his societal super structure) is supported
  3. MO7.3 Discuss the connection between support systems and the symptoms that often lead to complications such as PTSD.
  4. MO7.4 Assess the ongoing work of other students’ working Anthology Project.
  5. MO7.5 Produce brainstorming information for your Anthology Project.

Learning Activities

  1. Read historical background, summary, and lecture for Bhagavad Gita, and the text of the Bhagavad Gita. Answer the question at the end of the text.

  2. This week, please view the following multimedia materials:

    • This video by Epified provides a briefer summary and analysis of the concepts of the Bhagavad Gita.

    • This video by the Sanskrit Channel provides a deeper summary and analysis of the Bhagavad Gita.
