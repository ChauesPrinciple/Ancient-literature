Assignment Redo (Late Assignments)

Redo:

Sometimes life gets in the way, we get overwhelmed, or a pandemic happens. Missing an assignment does not have to be the end. Likewise, sometimes it is the assignment itself. It is okay to feel lost or to find that you just cannot wrap your head around an assignment. However, we do not want to give up.

This class attempts to embody that idea: a mistake is not the end. To make up a missed assignment, please use this form. Please note that it is a critical step to acknowledge what went wrong and why, and to develop a strategy to complete it. This is not a free pass. You need to demonstrate your understanding of what you need to do differently this time!

Please see the assignment dropbox for the Redo form!
