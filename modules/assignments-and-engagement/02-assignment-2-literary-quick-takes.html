Assignment #2: Literary Quick Takes

Assignment #2: Literary Quick Takes

Instructions

Purpose:

The purpose of the discussion boards is to flesh out and respond to the text in a way that is contemporary and meaningful. It is the place to propose ideas and connections with the text that make your experience with them more meaningful.

Task:

Craft a thoughtful response that addresses the discussion question, as well as the multimedia source. Your response should be at least a paragraph, with complete sentences and a clear structure.

You also need to respond to at least three of your colleague's responses. These responses should not be simplistic and say things like, "I agree!", "Good Job", or "I like this". The task is to engage with many different ideas about the same text!

Criteria:

You need to craft one well-structured and proofread response to the discussion, and you need to craft three respectful and engaging responses to receive the full points for the discussion post.

For more instructions on this method, please see TILT Higher ED.

Learning Outcomes

This assignment supports the following learning outcomes:  CLO2, CLO3, CLO4.

Submission Options

This assignment must be submitted in one of the following formats:

Video

Audio File

Text

Submission Due Date and Location

The assignment must be submitted to: Literary Quick Takes forum

The assignment is due after every reading assignment, on Saturday by 11:59 p.m.

Learners can expect to receive feedback within one week of submission.

Please note late submissions will not be accepted if they are past the discussion end date.
