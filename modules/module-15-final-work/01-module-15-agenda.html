Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to:

  1. MO15.1 Generate credible and applicable research for the chosen Literary Analysis topic.
  2. MO15.2 Construct an effective critical analysis of a chosen text using peer reviewed, primary, and contemporary research.
  3. MO15.3 Produce a mock anthology based on the proposed topic, with all the essential elements of the assignment.
  4. (Honors Option) MO15.4 Create an ePortfolio Presentation combining your Anthology Project and Literary Analysis into a multimedia project.  

Learning Activities

  1. Complete: This week, before we bid adieu, please complete the Final Discussion. This feedback is important to the ongoing efficacy of the course, and I appreciate it. 
  2. Submit: In our final week, we need to submit our final documents. This includes the Literary Analysis Essay and the Anthology Project. These documents are the final for this course. 

Reflect & Discuss

Engage with the text and your peers using the reading questions above.
