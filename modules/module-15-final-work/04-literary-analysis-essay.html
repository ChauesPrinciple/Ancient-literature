Literary Analysis Essay

Literary Analysis (Final)

Due: See your specific course calendar.

Purpose

Demonstrate the contemporary relevance of one ancient text from our course by connecting it to a clearly defined modern issue, problem, or situation. This essay functions as a forward for your Anthology Project: the single text you analyze here must also be included in your final Anthology.

Requirements (to earn credit)

  • Length: 3+ full pages (not counting the Works Cited page)

  • Sources: At least 2 outside scholarly/critical sources (plus the primary text)

  • Documentation: MLA in-text citations and a Works Cited page

  • Thesis: Clear argumentative claim linking the ancient text to a specific modern issue

  • Integration: Close reading of the text + application to the chosen contemporary source/issue
