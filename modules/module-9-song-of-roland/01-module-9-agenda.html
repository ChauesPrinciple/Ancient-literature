Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to: 

  1. MO9.1 Investigate the role of propaganda in war and the use of narrative storytelling to persuade warriors into allegiance
  2. MO9.2 Correlate and compare ancient methods of propaganda with modern examples
  3. MO9.3 Examine how the use of propaganda can lead to “bad faith,” or a shrinkage of moral horizons according to Dr. Shay.
  4. MO9.4 Compare the values of the Frankish society that produced the Song of Roland with that of the ancient Greeks.
  5. MO9.5 Assemble the notes, research, and content generated for your research projects.
  6. MO9.6 Summarize your research consultation with a librarian.

Learning Activities

  1. Read: Historical background, summary, and lecture for the Song of Roland 
  2. Read the Song of Roland.  Answer the question at the end of the text.
