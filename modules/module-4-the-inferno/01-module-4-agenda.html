Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to:

  1. MO4.1 Analyze a specific passage of the Inferno and engage critically with the different methods of reading the text (this concept is introduced in the lecture for The Inferno). (CO 1, 2, 3)
  2. MO4.2 Practice the skill of Close Reading by examining a chosen passage of the Inferno using the Close Reading handout (CO 1, 3)  
  3. MO4.3 Identify the relationship between Dante’s reaction to sinners and explore why Dante sympathizes with those sinners. (CO 1, 2, 3, 4)    
  4. MO4.4 differentiate between different methods of reading a text (outlined in the lecture of the Inferno) (CO 1, 2, 3)
  5. MO4.5 Discuss the implications of Virgil’s advice to Dante about how he should treat the sinners. (CO 1, 2, 4)
