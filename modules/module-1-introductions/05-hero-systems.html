Hero Systems

Assessment Note

This reading includes an assessment component.

Becker makes the claim that every culture is involved in creating "heroic systems", and he distinguishes between "high" and "low" heroism. Focusing on historical figures that we view as heroes is not the point; he is asking us to see the "systems" that entrench a worldview that denies reality.

"What forms of 'low' heroism are perpetuated in your real life?"

This can be through family, television, social media, etc. List an experience you have had with these ideals of "heroism". Engage with what Becker determines to be the most liberating question of all:

Key Question

How empirically true is the cultural hero system that sustains and drives you?

Is he correct that the youth no longer feel in heroism "the plan"?
