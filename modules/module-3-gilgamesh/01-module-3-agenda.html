Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to:

  1. MO3.1 Evaluate student responses to the  Chapter Theory discussion (opened in week one) by responding to other students. (CO 2, 4)
  2. MO3.2 relate your understanding of  the concept of the Hero’s Journey by applying it to your example  (CO 1, 2, 3, 4)
  3. MO3.3 Produce a summary of Gilgamesh (CO 1, 2)
  4. MO3.4 Apply the Hero’s Journey to the story of Gilgamesh, the first heroic epic (CO 1, 3, 4)

Learning Activities

  1. Read the Epic of Gilgamesh, as well as the accompanying Background, Summary, and Analysis. Complete the question at the end of the section.

  2. This week, please view the following multimedia materials:

    • This video provided by Ted-Ed provides a nice summary of the work as well as some commentary that is relevant to our analysis.
    1. Deep Dive: Flood Narratives
      1. Noah and the Near East Great Flood,
      2. Yu and the Chinese Great Flood, and
      3. The Indigenous American Great Flood
  3. Complete: This week you need to complete the Literary Quick Take for Gilgamesh and make sure you have responded to other students for the Chapter Theory Discussion for Chapter One.

Reflect & Discuss

Engage with the text and your peers using the reading questions above.
