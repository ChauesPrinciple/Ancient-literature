Gilgamesh

﻿

Gilgamesh is the quintessential hero; he is the first recorded hero. He is also the prototype for Joseph Campbell's Heroic Journey and Ernest Becker's Heroic Systems; it is also obvious how these theories apply once we consider it.

The first part, the Departure, includes the following stages:

  1. The Status Quo
  2. The Call to Adventure
  3. Assistance
  4. Departure

The second part, the Initiation, includes the following stages:

  1. Trials
  2. Approach to the Innermost Cave
  3. Crisis
  4. Treasure
  5. Result

The third part, the Return, includes the following stages:

  1. Return
  2. Resurrection
